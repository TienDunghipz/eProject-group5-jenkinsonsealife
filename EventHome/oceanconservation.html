High School Students Earn 3 College Credits at the Aquarium!

Jenkinson Sea Life has partnered with Orange Coast College (OCC) to offer a new course Ocean Conservation and Community Science Course for High School Students for 3 transferable college credits. Instruction and Labs will take place in-person and on-site at the Aquarium Fall Semester, Tuesday and Thursday Evenings, 8:00p.m. – 6:00p.m.

The program is limited to 30 students.

He course will be taught by the Jenkinson President and CEO, Dr. Peter Kareiva and Jenkinson staff experts. The Course is FREE for High School Students and the Aquarium will reimburse the $52 registration fees to any High School student enrolled in the course. Students need to enroll directly with Orange Coast
