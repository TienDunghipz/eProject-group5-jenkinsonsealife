Southeast Asia Day

The Aquarium of the Pacific will celebrate its eighteenth annual Southeast Asia Day.

The festival’s program will highlight the beauty and diversity of the cultures of Cambodia, Thailand, Vietnam, Myanmar, Laos, and Indonesia. Guests can enjoy music, dance, and cultural craft demonstrations.

Take your magical ride through the largest tropical aquarium
The Jenkinson is the largest, cylindrical, free-standing aquarium in the world. It is unique in its construction and therefore an absolute highlight. Around 1,500 fish from over 100 different species live in the tropical tank. If you look closely, you can discover Nemo, Dory and many other colorful fish up close.
You will receive precise information and details about the individual fish from our staff during the ride.
The first ride starts at 11 a.m. The last ride starts at 6:45 p.m. The trip is only possible with a ticket for Jenkinson Sea Life. We do not offer AquaDom trips without entry to Jenkinson Sea Life.

Dive into the fascinating underwater world at Jenkinson Sea Life and enjoy unique and exciting insights into the beautiful world of the seas. Discover over 5000 fascinating creatures in 37 lifelike freshwater and saltwater pools. Follow the course of the water from the sources of the river Spree to the depths of the Atlantic.

Diving goggles on and fins on - say "Hello" to our fascinating underwater inhabitants. Learn exciting stories from our staff about the world of swimming, snapping and pinching.
